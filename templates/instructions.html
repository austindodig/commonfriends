Game Instructions

Welcome to the game! Here are the rules:

  • Each round has a judge who selects a target player.
  • The targeted player chooses one friend from a list of 5.
  • The judge must guess which friend the targeted player picked.
  • Correct guesses earn points for the judge, incorrect guesses earn points for the targeted player.
  • The game ends when a player reaches 10 points.
