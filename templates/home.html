Game Instructions

Setup:


Agree on a "Friends" List: Players create a list of people known by everyone â€” friends, family, coworkers, or celebrities!

Add More for Fun: The more names, the better! Five random names are used each round.

Gameplay:


Beginning of Round: Player 1 becomes the judge for the first round, and will rotate each round. The judge selects a player to target. If there are only two players, this step is automatically skipped.

Target Player's Guess The targeted player reads the question and picks the friend it fits best.

Judge's Guess: The judge guesses who the target picked.

Scoring:

Judge is correct? The judge earns a point.

Judge is incorrect? The targeted player earns a point.

Winning:


First to 10 points wins!

Rules:


Be honest, grab your favorite beverage, and have fun!
