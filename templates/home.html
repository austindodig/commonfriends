Welcome to Common Friends!

Forget the facts... How well do you know people?




Or Join an Existing Game:





Gameplay:

- Players agree on a list of people who are known by all players. These can be friends, family, coworkers, celebrities, or anyone else!

- The more the merrier! Adding more common friends to the list makes the game more interesting, as 5 random friends will be used for each round.

- On a player's turn, they choose another player to target. The targeted player reads the question and selects the friend who the question applies to the most.

- The judge then guesses who the targeted player selected.

- If the judge is correct, the judge is awarded a point.

- If the judge is incorrect, the targeted player gets the point.

- The first player to 10 points wins the game.

- Be Honest in your answers!

- Grab your favorite beverage and have a great time with your friends!
